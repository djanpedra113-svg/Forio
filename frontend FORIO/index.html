Welcome to Forio

Within cybersecurity the largest vulnerability is human error, therefore it is crucial that employees go through the proper training to minimise risk. The aim of Forio is to provide employees with a better understanding of forensic readiness through the use of modules, quizzes, scores and feedback. Forensic readiness is a vital aspect of cybersecurity, as it ensures that in the event of an incident organisations can properly respond and investigate cyberattacks. With the advancement of technology, organisations are facing an increased amount of cybersecurity threats, ultimately leading to data loss, reputation damage and financial losses. It is important that organisations have a strong foundation in forensic readiness to mitigate these risks, by being able to correctly identify, collect, store and preserve evidence. Forio allows employees to learn more about forensic readiness in a digestible format, improving their knowledge and ensuring it meets organisational standards.
