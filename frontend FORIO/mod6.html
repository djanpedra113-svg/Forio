Applying forensic readiness

It is important that forensic readiness is integrated into an organisation's cybersecurity strategy and that departments work together to support incident investigations and protect digital evidence.

Departments

  • IT team
  • Management
  • Security team
  • Legal team

Challenges Organisations Face:

  • Lack of training
  • Limited budgets
  • Weak Security procedures
  • Poor record keeping

Business Continuity

Business continuity is an organisations ability to continue operating during and after disruptions such as cyber attacks. Organisations with strong forensic readiness recover more effectively, ultimately reducing downtime.

Continuous Improvement

Organisations should continue to improve their forensic readiness by, updating their policies, training employees regularly and reviewing incidents to learn from them. Quiz
